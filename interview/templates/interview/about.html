About IntelliView

An AI-powered mock interview platform built to help developers practice, get real feedback, and walk into interviews with confidence.

What is IntelliView?

IntelliView is a mock interview coach that generates role-specific technical questions using AI and evaluates your answers in real time. You get structured feedback — what you did well, where you fell short, and exactly how to improve.

It's built for developers who want to practice without the pressure of a real interview, and get honest feedback instead of generic tips.

How It Works

1
Pick a Role

Choose from 25+ tech roles including Django, FastAPI, React, DevOps and more.

2
AI Generates Questions

15 role-specific questions are generated fresh for every session using a large language model.

3
Answer in 30 Minutes

You have 30 minutes to answer all questions, just like a real timed technical interview.

4
Get Feedback

Every answer gets scored and broken down into strengths, weaknesses, and specific improvements.
